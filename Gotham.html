Overview

This font was released in 2000 by Tobias Frere-Jones and Jesse Ragan. It is an sans-serif font with OpenType features such as tabular numerics and it supports many languages such as Latin, Cyrillic and Greek.

Usage

Gotham font went very popular with the Freedom Tower using this typeface in 2004, and in 2008 with Obama presidential campanign. It is the typeface of of many compaines such as Asda (since 2007).
